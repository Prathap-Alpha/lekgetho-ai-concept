Smart Capture — photograph documents, the forms fill themselves

An officer photographs a client's receipt, Omang or payslip. Lekgetho AI reads it and auto-populates the official BURS forms — ITW8, ITW7A, ITW10 and VAT 002 — ready to review and submit. One photo serves every form.
